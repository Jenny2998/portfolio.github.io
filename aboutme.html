A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Jennifer Luisa Villar Polanco and I am a Graphic Designer from Boston. I love typography and color theory. I enjoy challenging myself in every way possible as well as learning new things with every experience. Lastly, I try to incorporate my culture and where I came from in every design, that way I bring another perspective and point of view to the each project.
